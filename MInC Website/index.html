Mobile Music Making

The field of mobile music making is exploring the use of smartphone as a musical instruments. The smartphone interface and features including gestures, sensors, touchscreen and Internet capabilities provide utility in creating a collective musical performance.

Inspiration

Originally inspired by Terry Riley's "In C", MInC is a collaborative music making application designed for iOS devices used alongside an application developed in the Max programming environment.

Collaborative Experiece

Players can drop out and listen or join in and advance to the next module at will and have the ability to change octave, speed, note duration, pitch and instrument with the push of a button.
